Loyal Points

Join our loyalty rewards program to start earning points on purchases over 100 Kshs.
Simply provide your phone number when checking out and we will automatically add points to your account based on your qualified purchases.
The more you shop with us, the more points you earn!
For every 100 Kshs spent in store, you will receive 1 loyalty point.
Points can be redeemed for goods on future purchases on the 1st to 10th of each month.
